Tournament Venue

The tournament takes place at the Gaststätte-Pension Götzfried (Doanustraße 13). All participants can stay at a single room directly at the venue for 45€ per night (double rooms are cheaper). Since we can only use the venue if enough participants book a room in advance, we'd like to encourage you to reserve a room as early as you can. Please use this link for booking and include the keyword "Diplomacy". If there are any questions, feel free to write me via robschuppe [at] posteo.de!
