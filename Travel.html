Tournament Venue

On Saturday, the tournament takes place at the Gaststätte-Pension Götzfried (Donaustraße 13, 93105 Tegernheim). All participants can stay at a single room directly at the venue for 45€ per night (double rooms are cheaper). On Sunday, we'll play at the Gaststätte im Sportpark Schwabelweis (Donaustaufer Str. 260, 93055 Regensburg). If there are any questions, feel free to write me via robschuppe [at] posteo.de!
