RegensCon 2026

A Diplomacy tournament held on June 20th and 21th in Regensburg, Germany. Come and join us for three rounds of Diplomacy in a friendly environment! There will be also the opportunity for a guided tour through the ciy, a selection of other board games, and meeting new people with similar interests.
